Rules

  1. On a turn, a player rolls the die repeatedly, to accumulate as many points as possible.
  2. However, if a player rolls a 1, the player's turn is over and any points they have accumulated during this turn are lost.
  3. A player can also choose to hold (stop rolling the die) and all of the points rolled during that turn are added to their score.
  4. When a player reaches a total of 100 or more points, the game ends and that player is the winner.
